Popular Festivals

Pinagbenga Festival

Baguio City

Panagbenga Festival, A vibrant celebration showcasing flower floats, street dancing, and cultural events, highlighting Baguio's flower industry and heritage, with parades, performances, and exhibitions that bring the city to life.

Lucban, Quezon

Pahiyas Festival, A colorful harvest festival honoring San Isidro Labrador, featuring elaborate decorations made from fruits, vegetables, and other local products, with processions, music, and feasting, showcasing the town's gratitude and festive spirit.

Ilo-ilo City

Dinagyang Festival, A grand celebration honoring the Santo Niño and the Ati people, featuring tribal dances, music, and colorful costumes, with participants competing in street dancing and float parades, showcasing Iloilo's rich cultural heritage and traditions.
